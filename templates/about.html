About Naeilum

내일:음 - Tomorrow's Sound

Our Mission

Naeilum bridges cultures by helping international friends discover meaningful Korean names that resonate with their identity. Our name means "Tomorrow's Sound" (내일:음), representing the harmony between your heritage and Korean culture.

How It Works

  • Personalized Selection: We analyze your name's initial and characteristics to find matching Korean names
  • Meaningful Names: Each name comes with its Hanja (Chinese characters) and deep cultural meaning
  • Cultural Categories: Names are organized by themes like Wisdom, Courage, Beauty, and more
  • Daily Fortune: Get a unique fortune based on your Korean name that changes every day

Name Categories

Our Korean names are carefully curated and categorized by their meanings:

  • Wisdom (지혜): Names representing intelligence and understanding
  • Courage (용기): Names symbolizing bravery and strength
  • Beauty (아름다움): Names reflecting grace and elegance
  • Hope (희망): Names carrying wishes for a bright future
  • Harmony (조화): Names emphasizing balance and peace
  • Love (사랑): Names expressing warmth and affection

Cultural Significance

In Korean culture, names carry deep meaning and are often chosen based on:

  • The meaning of Hanja characters
  • Sound harmony and flow
  • Wishes and hopes for the person's future
  • Family traditions and values
