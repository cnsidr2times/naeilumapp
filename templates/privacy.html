Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2024

Our Commitment to Privacy

Naeilum is designed with privacy in mind. We believe in providing a fun and meaningful service without compromising your personal information.

Information We Collect

What we collect:

  • The name you enter (temporarily, during your session only)
  • Your gender preference for the Korean name
  • Your Korean name selection

What we DON'T collect:

  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Location data
  • IP addresses for tracking
  • Any other personal identifiable information

How We Use Information

The information you provide is used solely to:

  • Generate Korean name recommendations
  • Display your selected name and its meaning
  • Generate daily fortunes based on your Korean name

Data Storage

  • Session Storage: Your inputs are stored temporarily in your browser session
  • No Permanent Storage: We do not save any personal data to databases
  • Anonymous Usage: All interactions are completely anonymous
  • Auto-Deletion: Session data is automatically cleared when you close your browser

Cookies

We use only essential session cookies to maintain your experience while using the app. These cookies:

  • Are temporary and deleted when you close your browser
  • Don't track you across websites
  • Don't contain personal information
  • Are necessary for the app to function properly

Third-Party Services

Naeilum does not share any data with third-party services. We don't use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media plugins
  • External tracking tools

Children's Privacy

Naeilum is suitable for all ages. We don't knowingly collect any information from children under 13, as we don't collect personal information from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please reach out through our About page.
